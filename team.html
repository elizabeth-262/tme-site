Team Lead

Si Jia Wu

PhD Student, Developmental Psychology and Education

Si Jia holds a BA in Philosophy and a minor in Psychology from the University of Toronto and an MA in Developmental Psychology and Education from OISE. She has validated the use of Transdermal Optical Imaging for assessing stress and researched its applications for measuring physiological and psychological states. Currently, she continues her research on stress and explores children's emotional engagement with manga stories.
