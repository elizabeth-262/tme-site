Our Study

Information and Eligibility Criteria

In-person Teen Manga Study

Description

This study aims to understand how teenagers engage with manga stories. During the study, you will be given manga chapters to read and a survey to complete. More detailed information will be provided in our follow-up email after signing up.

Eligibility Criteria

- Between ages 13-17
- Have read manga before

Additional Information

Our lab is located at the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education in downtown Toronto. The study takes approximately 1-1.5 hours. Upon completion, participants will receive a free manga volume and travel reimbursement.
