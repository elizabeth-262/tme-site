About Us

Led by PhD student, Si Jia Wu, the Manga Engagement Study at the University of Toronto investigates the interactions between teenagers and Japanese comics (manga). The research seeks to understand how young readers engage with manga stories.

"Participating in the manga engagement study was an eye-opening experience. It was fascinating to see how in-depth analysis of manga can reveal such nuanced insights into reader engagement. Highly recommend it to anyone interested in manga and its impact!" - Participant/Si Jia
