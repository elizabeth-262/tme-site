Our Study

In-person Teen Manga Study

This study examines teenagers' engagement with manga. It's held synchronously at our OISE lab, and participants will receive a complimentary manga for taking part in the study.

About Us

Led by PhD student, Si Jia Wu, the Manga Engagement Study at the University of Toronto investigates the interactions between teenagers and Japanese comics. The research seeks to understand how young readers engage with manga stories.
